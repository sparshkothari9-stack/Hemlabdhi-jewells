About Hem Labdhi Jewels

India's trusted destination for premium fashion jewellery — serving B2B and B2C clients nationwide

Our Story

Crafting Elegance, Empowering Style

Hem Labdhi Jewels was founded with a singular vision — to bridge the gap between designer luxury and everyday affordability. What started as a passion project has grown into one of India's most trusted fashion jewellery brands, serving both individual customers and businesses across the country.

We specialize in handcrafted necklaces, crowns, brooches, earrings, kadas, bracelets, hand panjas and maang tikas — each piece designed to reflect contemporary trends while maintaining timeless elegance. Our collection of 310+ designs is curated for women who appreciate quality, craftsmanship, and style.
